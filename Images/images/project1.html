1. Introduction Problem Statement

Educational institutions often struggle to track and improve student performance due to fragmented data across exams, attendance, and extracurricular activities. The goal of this project was to combine multiple data points into a single interactive dashboard to identify key performance drivers and provide actionable insights.

2. Project Objectives

  • Identify factors influencing student exam performance
  • Measure the impact of attendance on academic results
  • Provide interactive tools for educators to filter by performance/attendance categories
  • Highlight students who need academic support
